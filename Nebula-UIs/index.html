Nebula

Welcome to Nebula! This website hosts a variety of related research projects focused on visualizing and interacting with high-dimensional data. Please click on one of the links below for more information about each part of Nebula.

Keep in mind that these pages are for demonstration and research purposes as opposed to being full-fledged and flawless tools. As such, we highly recommend using Google Chrome and the window at about 13" width or more to access these pages, but Safari is likely to work as well. Firefox has known issues with these pages. Similarly, having the window too small may make the page render incorrectly.

Have a question or notice a bug? Feel free to reach out and let us know!
